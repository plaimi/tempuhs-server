########
# LAWS #
########

FlexLaws
========
1. A timespan with a clock with a name of "<~>" is a flexible timespan and
subject to the laws of this section.

2. A flexible timespan's beginMin is equal to the smallest beginMin of all of
its immediate descendants' beginMin.

3. A flexible timespan's endMax is equal to the biggest endMax of all of its
immediate descendants' endMax.


ParentLaws
==========
1. A timespan may not have a descendant as a parent.

2. A timespan may not have itself as a parent.
